Our Story

Born from a love of food and a dream to bring India's best flavours to your doorstep — fast, fresh, and full of heart.

🍽️ Who We Are

PipaliyaPlates was founded in 2025 with one bold mission — to bring flavor, freshness, and fast delivery together on a single sizzling platform. We believe that great food should never be hard to reach. Whether you're craving a hearty Gujarati Thali, spicy Punjabi Chole Bhature, cheesy Italian pizza, or smoky Korean Bibimbap — our chefs prepare it all with passion and care.

We started as a small kitchen in Ahmedabad with just 3 dishes on the menu. Today, we serve 25+ dishes across 8 cuisines, with thousands of happy orders delivered every month. Every plate that leaves our kitchen carries a piece of our heart — because that's what food means to us.
